Assignment 1: Company Logistics Process

This assignment explores the basics of logistics and how companies implement it for better operations.

Main Components of Logistics

  • Sourcing of Raw Material
  • Storage (Warehouse)
  • Transportation
  • Information & Control Systems
  • Supply Chain
  • Inventory Management
  • Order Fulfillment
  • Demand
  • Reverse Logistics
  • Customer Service
  • Laziness of People

Why Do Companies Invest in Logistics?

  • Cost reduction and efficiency
  • Customer satisfaction and competitive advantage
  • Supply chain resilience (risk management)
  • Scalability and expansion
  • Environmental sustainability
  • That's the world's demand in order to compete and run a business

Why Is It Important?

  • Improves supply chain efficiency
  • Enhances customer comfort

What Is the Impact on Competition?

Market prices and trends affect how companies manage logistics. For example, if shoe demand goes up, brands like NIKE will have to compete harder for materials, which could cause shortages. This changes their logistics approach. Better logistics mean better prices, faster delivery, and a stronger market position.

Current and Future Trends

  • Automation and AI (smart warehouses, robotics)
  • 3D Printing (future)
  • Sustainable logistics (e.g., FedEx plans to be carbon-neutral by 2040)
  • Real-world examples: CJ Logistics, Coupang Rocket Delivery
